Privacy Policy for tbreakapp

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to tbreakapp ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information when you create an account:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Username or display name
  • Profile information you choose to provide

2.2 Usage Data

We collect data about your use of the app, including:

  • Daily check-in entries (mood, notes, progress)
  • Calendar data (40-day tracking)
  • Chat messages in community rooms
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the tbreakapp service
  • Track your 40-day progress and display your calendar
  • Enable community chat features
  • Send push notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your requests and support inquiries

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase/Firestore: Industry-standard cloud database with encryption
  • Local Storage: Offline data stored encrypted on your device
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with secure password hashing

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Community Features: Your username and chat messages are visible to other users in community chat rooms
  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google Cloud) for hosting and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in app settings

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud messaging
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment processing (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

8. Children's Privacy

tbreakapp is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account at any time, after which your data will be deleted within 30 days.

10. Offline Data

Our app works offline. Data created offline is stored locally on your device and automatically synced to our servers when you reconnect to the internet. Local data is protected by your device's security measures.

11. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for:

  • Daily check-in reminders
  • Milestone achievements
  • Community chat messages

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
